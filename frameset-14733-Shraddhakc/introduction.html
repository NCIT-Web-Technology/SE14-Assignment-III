HYATT RESORT

Hyatt Regency Kathmandu is an urban five-star resort located near Boudhanath Stupa, a UNESCO world heritage site, in Kathmandu, Nepal. The hotel opened on 27 September 2000 is built on 37 acres of land. It is a full service hotel and the only Hyatt brand property in Nepal under the Hyatt Regency portfolio.Hyatt Regency Kathmandu is surrounded by the magnificent Himalayan Mountains. The 280 room city resort occupies ground overlooking the city of Kathmandu. The hotel is adjacent to Boudhanath Stupa, the most revered of Tibetan Buddhist shrines outside of Tibet, just ten minutes from the Tribhuvan International Airport.The hotel's top 3 floors are Regency Club rooms and suites with Regency Club lounge situated in the 6th floor.
