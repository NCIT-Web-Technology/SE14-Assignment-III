Universities In Nepal

At present, higher education in Nepal is offered by six universities: Tribhuwan University (TU), Mahendra Sanskrit University (MSU), Lumbini Bauddha University (LBU), Kathmandu University (KU), Purbanchal University (PurU) and Pokhara University (PokhU). In addition to these, there are two more academic institutions viz. BP Koirala Institute of Health Science (BPKIHS) and National Academy of Medical Science (NAMS) that run medical courses of undergraduate and post graduate levels. There are also other institutions such as Higher secondary education board (HSEB) and Center for Technical Education and Vocational Training (CTEVT) that run higher education program both at liberal arts and technical education
